Ram Mandir: A Grand Temple in the Heart of Pune

Ram mandir Pune: Pune, known as the cultural capital of Maharashtra, is home to numerous temples that showcase the city's rich cultural and spiritual heritage. One of the most iconic temples in Pune is the Ram Mandir located in the bustling Pune Camp area. This magnificent temple is dedicated to Lord Ram and sees thousands of devotees flocking daily to offer prayers and seek blessings.

Sinhagad : An ancient hill fortress

The fort was captured by Tanaji Malusare and his brother. Sinhagad (Lion's Fort) fort was strategically built to provide natural protection from the enemies due to its very steep slopes.The walls of the forts and bastions were constructed only at key places. There are two gates to enter the fort named Kalyan Darwaja and Pune Darwaza.
